In the previous lesson, we explored Literal And Variable Patterns. Now we are going to take a look at Constructor Patterns, which is an important concept in Krypton. As you may already be aware, many programming languages have some way of talking about matching on a variant constructor and binding the data carried inside it, and Krypton is no exception to that general pattern. It is worth mentioning, however, that the exact spelling still matters, because small syntax choices tend to shape how comfortably a reader can move through later code.
